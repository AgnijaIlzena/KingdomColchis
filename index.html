Welcome to the Royal Kingdom of Colchis

The role of the Royal Family of Colchis

Members of the Royal Family support The King in his many State and national duties, as well as carrying out important work in the areas of public and charitable service, and helping to strengthen national unity and stability.

Every year the Royal Family as a whole carries out over 1,000 official engagements throughout the Colchis and worldwide.

When official events such as receptions, State banquets and garden parties are held, the Royal Family supports The King in making her guests welcome.

The Royal Family also plays an important role in recognising and supporting the work of the Armed Services. Members of the Royal Family have official relationships with many units of the Forces, paying regular visits to soldiers, sailors and airmen serving at home and abroad.

Finally, the Royal Family as a whole plays a role in strengthening national unity. Members of the Royal Family are able to recognise and participate in community and local events in every part of the Colchis, from the opening of new buildings to celebrations or acts of commemoration.
